Pricing Breakdown

  • Free$0

  • Enterprise$39/mo
  • Broadest IDE support in the market
  • Native GitHub integration (PR reviews, issues)

Best for: Power users who want the most advanced AI features
  • Hobby$0
  • Pro$20/mo
  • Business$40/mo
  • Composer: multi-file AI editing across codebase
  • Choose your model per request
  • Full codebase indexing and context
  • More expensive but more powerful AI features

The Verdict

Choose GitHub Copilot if: You use multiple IDEs (especially JetBrains or Xcode), your team is on GitHub, you want the cheapest paid option, or you need enterprise features like fine-tuning.

Choose Cursor if: You want the most advanced multi-file editing, you prefer choosing AI models per request, you work primarily in a VS Code-style editor, or you need deep codebase-aware suggestions.

Budget pick: Copilot Pro at $10/mo is hard to beat for solo developers. For teams, Copilot Business ($19) vs Cursor Business ($40) is a significant cost difference.
