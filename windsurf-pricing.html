Windsurf raised Pro from $15 to $20 in March 2026 and switched from credits to daily/weekly quotas. The key differentiator: FedRAMP + HIPAA compliance that no competitor matches.

Quota System — Daily/Weekly Rate Limits

In March 2026, Windsurf replaced its credit pool with daily and weekly quotas. Instead of a monthly budget, you get a daily allowance that refreshes every 24 hours.

What ChangedOld System (Credits)New System (Quotas)
Billing unit Monthly credit pool Daily/weekly rate limits
Flexibility High — use anytime in month Lower — daily caps
Predictability Can run out mid-month Consistent daily access
Overage Service stops Wait for next day refresh

Community reaction: Mixed. Power users who preferred bursty usage (burning credits in intense coding sessions) dislike the daily caps. Steady users benefit from guaranteed daily access.
